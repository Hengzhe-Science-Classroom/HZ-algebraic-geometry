# Algebraic Geometry · Interactive

**Varieties, schemes, and sheaves**

**Status:** Planned

Part of [Hengzhe's Science Classroom](https://hengzhe-science-classroom.github.io/hub/) · Tier 5 · Research Frontier

## What You'll Learn

- Affine and projective varieties
- Hilbert's Nullstellensatz
- Sheaves and schemes
- Morphisms and dimension
- Divisors and line bundles
- Cohomology of sheaves

## References

- Robin Hartshorne, *Algebraic Geometry* (GTM 52, Springer)
- Ravi Vakil, *The Rising Sea: Foundations of Algebraic Geometry* (free online)
- Igor R. Shafarevich, *Basic Algebraic Geometry* (Vols. 1 & 2, Springer)

## Features (when built)

- **Interactive visualizations** — every concept comes alive with draggable, animated demos
- **KaTeX math rendering** — rigorous notation throughout
- **Exercises with hints & solutions** — from computational to proof-based
- **Progress tracking** — saved locally in your browser
- **Zero dependencies** — just open the link and learn
